## 2-Minute Loom Recording Script

Record your screen walking through these steps. Keep it under 2 minutes.

### Part 1: Patient Side (60 seconds)

1. Open the Sunrise Clinic patient page
2. Show the clinic homepage — name, address, timings
3. Click "Services" — show the list with fees
4. Click "Doctors" — show doctor profiles and availability
5. Click "Ask Us" — type "What are your timings?" and show the response
6. Type "What is the consultation fee?" — show the fee response
7. Click "Book Appointment" — fill in a sample booking
8. Show the success page

**Say:** "This is what your patients see. They can check your timings, see your fees, ask questions, and book appointments — all from their phone. No app download."

### Part 2: Admin Side (60 seconds)

1. Go to the login page, log in as clinic admin
2. Show the dashboard overview
3. Show the appointments list — point out the new booking from Part 1
4. Click "Confirm" on the appointment
5. Quickly show Doctors page, Services page, FAQs page
6. Show that timings and rules are configurable

**Say:** "This is your dashboard. You see every appointment request, and you can confirm or reject. You control your doctors, services, fees, timings, and FAQs. Everything your patients see comes from what you set up here."

### Closing (10 seconds)

**Say:** "I can set this up for your clinic in 10 minutes. First 3 months free. Interested? Send me a message."

## In-Person Demo Flow (5 minutes at a clinic)

If you walk into a clinic and get 5 minutes with the doctor or manager:

1. **Open the patient page on your phone** — show them the Sunrise Clinic demo
2. **Say:** "This is what your patients would see for YOUR clinic"
3. **Show the assistant** — type a question, show the instant response
4. **Show booking** — "Patients book here, you see it in your dashboard"
5. **Switch to admin** — log in, show the dashboard with appointments
6. **Close:** "I can set this up with YOUR details in 10 minutes. Free for 3 months. All I need is your doctor list, timings, and fees."

**If they say yes:** Pull out your phone/laptop and create their tenant on the spot using the super admin panel.

**If they say "let me think":** Send them the WhatsApp message with the demo link.

## What You Need From Each Clinic (Onboarding Checklist)

When a clinic agrees, collect this info (can be via WhatsApp):

- [ ] Clinic name
- [ ] Address
- [ ] Phone number
- [ ] Email
- [ ] Clinic timings (open days, hours, breaks)
- [ ] Doctor names + specializations
- [ ] Doctor availability (days + time slots)
- [ ] Consultation fees
- [ ] List of services with fees and durations
- [ ] 5-6 common patient questions and answers
- [ ] Do they want manual approval for appointments? (Y/N)
- [ ] Do they allow same-day booking? (Y/N)
- [ ] Do they allow walk-ins? (Y/N)
