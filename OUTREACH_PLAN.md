# Tenivra Outreach Plan — Hyderabad

## Target: 3 Pilot Clinics in 2 Weeks

### Ideal Clinic Profile

- Small clinic (1-3 doctors)
- No existing online booking system (no Practo, no website with booking)
- Has a receptionist who answers phone calls all day
- Located in a residential area with walk-in patients
- Specialties: General Physician, Dental, Dermatology, ENT, Pediatrics, Physiotherapy

### Search Strategy

#### Google Maps Searches (do these from your phone)

Search each of these in Google Maps. Look for clinics with:
- High ratings but NO website or booking link
- Phone number listed (means they rely on calls)
- Reviews mentioning "long wait" or "call to book" (your product solves this)

#### JustDial / Practo Research

1. Go to justdial.com, search "clinics in [area]"
2. Filter by those with ONLY a phone number (no website)
3. These are your best targets — they have zero digital presence

#### Personal Network

- Ask family members which clinic they go to
- Ask friends if they know any clinic owners
- College connections — anyone in healthcare?

### Common Objections and Responses

**"We don't need this, we have enough patients"**
→ "This isn't to get more patients. It's to reduce phone calls and help existing patients book without calling. Your receptionist will thank you."

**"Our patients are not tech-savvy"**
→ "It works on any phone browser — no app to download. If they can use WhatsApp, they can use this."

**"How much does it cost?"**
→ "Free for the first 3 months while we improve the product. After that, the basic plan will be very affordable — around ₹500-1000/month."

**"Show me first"**
→ Open the demo on your phone immediately. This is why you have the Sunrise Clinic seeded.

**"I need to ask the doctor"**
→ "No problem. Can I send you a 2-minute video demo on WhatsApp? You can forward it to the doctor."

**"We already use Practo"**
→ Skip this clinic. Move to the next one. Don't compete with Practo for now.
